In this unit, we will dive into the concept of focus, an essential aspect of photography. You will learn about different focus modes and how to achieve clarity in your photos.

Understanding Focus

Focus is the process of making adjustments to ensure that the subject of your photo is sharp and clear. You can achieve focus manually by turning the focus ring on your lens, or automatically by using your camera’s auto-focus feature.

Understanding Clarity

Clarity refers to the overall sharpness and definition of your photo. Ensuring your images are clear involves not only proper focus but also avoiding camera shake and using appropriate settings for the lighting conditions.
